PROBLEM STATEMENT

How might we redesign our digital and social environments to help people build deeper, more meaningful connections despite the challenges of maintaining contact with old friends and creating ones?

OVERVIEW

We reimagined Instagram's direct messaging space with Ties & Topics, two new features that revamp Instagram Notes and organizes users' chats. Using Instagram's algorithm, Ties presents shared interests and experiences to users to create connections, while agentive AI finds keywords in chats to

create topics that compiles relevant dates, plans, or pictures to be easily accessible. Users' plans will finally make it out of the group chat and encourage more in-person interactions.
